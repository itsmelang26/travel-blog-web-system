Discover the Philippines

From white sand beaches and majestic mountains to vibrant cities and cultural wonders, discover why the Philippines is truly paradise.

Boracay

Famous for its powdery white sand and crystal-clear waters, Boracay is a beach lover’s dream.

Chocolate Hills

Bohol’s iconic natural wonder with more than 1,200 perfectly cone-shaped hills.

Mayon Volcano

Known as the “perfect cone,” Mayon Volcano is one of the most picturesque volcanoes in the world.

Events & Festivals in the Philippines

Celebrate the vibrant culture of the Philippines through its world-famous festivals and colorful traditions!

Sinulog Festival

Held every January in Cebu City, honoring the Santo Niño with street dancing, parades, and vibrant costumes.

Ati-Atihan Festival

Kalibo, Aklan’s January festival with tribal dances, colorful face paint, and lively drumbeats.

Pahiyas Festival

Lucban, Quezon’s May festival showcasing homes decorated with fruits, vegetables, and handicrafts.

Kadayawan Festival

Davao City’s August thanksgiving celebration with floral floats and street dancing.
